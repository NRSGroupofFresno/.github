## IRS / Tax Compliance

### Employer Tax Obligations

#### Payroll Taxes
- [ ] **Federal Income Tax Withholding** - Withhold from employee wages
- [ ] **Social Security Tax (FICA)** - 6.2% employer / 6.2% employee
- [ ] **Medicare Tax** - 1.45% employer / 1.45% employee
- [ ] **Federal Unemployment Tax (FUTA)** - 6% on first $7,000 (usually 0.6% after credits)

#### Filing Requirements
| Form | Description | Due Date |
|------|-------------|----------|
| Form 941 | Quarterly Payroll Tax Return | End of month after quarter |
| Form 940 | Annual FUTA Return | January 31 |
| Form W-2 | Wage Statements to Employees | January 31 |
| Form W-3 | W-2 Transmittal to SSA | January 31 |
| Form 1099-NEC | Non-Employee Compensation | January 31 |
| Form 1096 | 1099 Transmittal | January 31 |

#### Deposit Schedules
- **Monthly Depositor:** Due by 15th of following month
- **Semi-Weekly Depositor:** Wed-Fri payroll = Wed deposit; Sat-Tue payroll = Fri deposit
- **One-Day Rule:** $100,000+ in taxes = next business day

### Business Income Taxes
- [ ] **Annual Tax Return** - Form 1120 (C-Corp), 1120-S (S-Corp), 1065 (Partnership), Schedule C (Sole Prop)
- [ ] **Estimated Tax Payments** - Quarterly (Apr 15, Jun 15, Sep 15, Jan 15)
- [ ] **Extension Filing** - Form 7004 if needed

## Department of Labor (DOL) Compliance

### Fair Labor Standards Act (FLSA)
- [ ] **Minimum Wage** - Federal: $7.25/hr (CA is higher, follow state)
- [ ] **Overtime** - 1.5x regular rate for hours over 40/week
- [ ] **Child Labor** - Age and hour restrictions
- [ ] **Recordkeeping** - Maintain payroll records 3 years

### Employee Classification
- [ ] **Exempt vs Non-Exempt** - Salary basis + duties test
- [ ] **Employee vs Independent Contractor** - IRS 20-factor test
- [ ] **Joint Employment** - Review staffing arrangements

### Family and Medical Leave Act (FMLA)
*Applies to employers with 50+ employees within 75 miles*
- [ ] **Eligibility Tracking** - 12 months employed, 1,250 hours
- [ ] **Leave Entitlement** - Up to 12 weeks unpaid leave
- [ ] **Notice Requirements** - Post FMLA notice, provide notices to employees
- [ ] **Certification** - Medical certification procedures

## OSHA (Occupational Safety & Health)

### General Requirements
- [ ] **OSHA Poster** - "Job Safety and Health" posted in workplace
- [ ] **Hazard Communication** - Written program, SDS, labeling, training
- [ ] **Emergency Action Plan** - Written plan if required
- [ ] **Fire Prevention Plan** - If required by OSHA standard

### Recordkeeping (10+ employees)
- [ ] **OSHA 300 Log** - Record of work-related injuries/illnesses
- [ ] **OSHA 300A Summary** - Post February 1 - April 30
- [ ] **OSHA 301 Forms** - Incident report forms
- [ ] **Retention** - Keep records 5 years

### Industry-Specific
*Check if these apply to your business:*
- [ ] Personal Protective Equipment (PPE)
- [ ] Lockout/Tagout
- [ ] Confined Spaces
- [ ] Fall Protection
- [ ] Respiratory Protection
- [ ] Bloodborne Pathogens

## Immigration (USCIS/ICE)

### Form I-9 Employment Eligibility
- [ ] **Complete I-9** - Within 3 business days of hire
- [ ] **Section 1** - Employee completes on/before first day
- [ ] **Section 2** - Employer completes within 3 days
- [ ] **Reverification** - When work authorization expires
- [ ] **Retention** - 3 years from hire OR 1 year after termination (whichever later)

### E-Verify (if applicable)
- [ ] Enroll in E-Verify if required (federal contractors, certain states)
- [ ] Submit verification within 3 days of I-9 completion
- [ ] Post required notices

## Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA)

### Employment (15+ employees)
- [ ] **Non-Discrimination** - Hiring, firing, promotions, pay
- [ ] **Reasonable Accommodation** - Interactive process
- [ ] **Medical Inquiries** - Limited to job-related
- [ ] **Confidentiality** - Medical records separate and confidential

### Public Accommodations
- [ ] **Accessibility** - Remove barriers where readily achievable
- [ ] **Service Animals** - Allow service dogs
- [ ] **Effective Communication** - Auxiliary aids if needed

---

## Equal Employment Opportunity (EEOC)

### Non-Discrimination Requirements
*Applies to employers with 15+ employees*
- [ ] **EEO Poster** - "EEO is the Law" posted
- [ ] **Title VII** - No discrimination based on race, color, religion, sex, national origin
- [ ] **Age Discrimination (ADEA)** - 40+ years old (20+ employees)
- [ ] **Genetic Information (GINA)** - No genetic information discrimination

### Reporting
- [ ] **EEO-1 Report** - Annual for 100+ employees (or 50+ with federal contracts)

---

## Federal Contractor Requirements
*If you have federal contracts or subcontracts:*

- [ ] **Affirmative Action Plans** - For contracts $50,000+
- [ ] **VETS-4212 Report** - Annual veteran reporting
- [ ] **E-Verify** - Mandatory for federal contractors
- [ ] **Davis-Bacon Act** - Prevailing wages on construction
- [ ] **Service Contract Act** - Prevailing wages on service contracts

---

## Privacy & Data Protection

### FTC Act
- [ ] **Privacy Policy** - If collecting personal information online
- [ ] **Data Security** - Reasonable security measures
- [ ] **Children's Privacy (COPPA)** - Under 13 requires parental consent

### HIPAA (if applicable)
- [ ] **Privacy Rule** - PHI protections
- [ ] **Security Rule** - Electronic PHI safeguards
- [ ] **Breach Notification** - Report breaches
