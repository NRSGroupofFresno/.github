## City of Fresno Business License

### General Business License Requirements

#### Who Needs a License
- [ ] Any person/entity conducting business within Fresno city limits
- [ ] Home-based businesses
- [ ] Contractors working in Fresno
- [ ] Temporary businesses and vendors

#### Application Process
1. [ ] Determine business type and location
2. [ ] Complete Business License Application
3. [ ] Pay applicable fees
4. [ ] Obtain any required secondary permits
5. [ ] Display license at place of business

#### Contact Information
- **City of Fresno Finance Department - Revenue Division**
- Address: 2600 Fresno Street, Room 2156, Fresno, CA 93721
- Phone: (559) 621-6880
- Website: https://www.fresno.gov/finance/business-license/

### License Types & Fees

| Business Type | Typical Fee Range |
|--------------|-------------------|
| General Business | Varies by gross receipts |
| Professional Services | Per professional |
| Contractor | Per contractor type |
| Home Occupation | Lower fee tier |
| Temporary/Special Event | Per event |

*Fees subject to change - verify current rates with City*

### Renewal Requirements
- [ ] **Annual Renewal** - Typically due by anniversary date or end of calendar year
- [ ] **Update Information** - Report any changes to business
- [ ] **Gross Receipts** - May need to report for fee calculation
- [ ] **Late Penalties** - Avoid by renewing on time

## Fresno Zoning & Land Use

### Planning & Development Department

#### Zoning Compliance
- [ ] Verify property is zoned for intended use
- [ ] Check for conditional use permit requirements
- [ ] Review parking requirements
- [ ] Understand signage restrictions

#### Common Zoning Categories
| Zone | Typical Uses |
|------|-------------|
| R-1, R-2, R-3 | Residential |
| C-1, C-2, C-3 | Commercial |
| C-M | Commercial/Light Manufacturing |
| M-1, M-2 | Industrial |
| P | Professional Office |

#### Home-Based Business Restrictions
- [ ] Limited to specific square footage
- [ ] No external evidence of business (usually)
- [ ] Parking restrictions
- [ ] No retail walk-in traffic (usually)
- [ ] Limited employees

#### Contact Information
- **Planning & Development Department**
- Address: 2600 Fresno Street, Room 3043
- Phone: (559) 621-8277
- Website: https://www.fresno.gov/darm/planning-development/

## Health Permits

### Fresno County Department of Public Health

#### Food Facility Permits
Required if you:
- [ ] Prepare, serve, or sell food
- [ ] Operate a restaurant, cafe, or food truck
- [ ] Sell food at farmers markets
- [ ] Operate a catering business

#### Permit Types
| Type | Description |
|------|-------------|
| Food Facility | Restaurants, cafes, markets |
| Temporary Food Facility | Events, fairs |
| Cottage Food Operation | Home-based (limited) |
| Mobile Food Facility | Food trucks, carts |

#### Requirements
- [ ] Submit application
- [ ] Pass plan review (new facilities)
- [ ] Pass health inspection
- [ ] Food handler certifications
- [ ] Annual renewal

#### Contact Information
- **Fresno County Environmental Health**
- Phone: (559) 600-3357
- Address: 1221 Fulton Street, Fresno, CA 93721
- Website: https://www.co.fresno.ca.us/departments/public-health/environmental-health

### Alcohol (ABC License)
