# California State Compliance Checklist
## State of California Regulatory Requirements

**Applicable to:** Businesses operating in Fresno, California
**Non-Attorney Advocacy Resource - For Educational Purposes Only**

---

## California Secretary of State

### Business Entity Compliance

#### Entity Formation & Maintenance
- [ ] **Statement of Information** - File within 90 days of formation, then:
  - Corporations: Annually
  - LLCs: Every 2 years
- [ ] **Agent for Service of Process** - Keep current
- [ ] **Business Name** - Fictitious Business Name (DBA) if applicable

| Entity Type | Filing | Due Date |
|-------------|--------|----------|
| Corporation | SI-550 | Anniversary month |
| LLC | LLC-12 | Every 2 years (by end of calendar month of formation) |
| LP/LLP | Various | Check specific requirements |

#### Filing Fees (as of 2024-2025)
- Statement of Information (Corp): $25
- Statement of Information (LLC): $20
- Fictitious Business Name: Varies by county

## Franchise Tax Board (FTB)

### State Income Tax

#### Filing Requirements
| Entity Type | Form | Due Date |
|-------------|------|----------|
| C Corporation | Form 100 | 15th day of 4th month after year end |
| S Corporation | Form 100S | 15th day of 3rd month after year end |
| LLC (taxed as partnership) | Form 568 | 15th day of 4th month after year end |
| Partnership | Form 565 | 15th day of 3rd month after year end |
| Sole Proprietor | Schedule CA (540) | April 15 |

#### Estimated Taxes
- [ ] **Quarterly Payments** - Due: April 15, June 15, September 15, January 15
- [ ] **Minimum Franchise Tax** - $800 (most entities)

#### LLC Fees (Annual)
| Total CA Income | Fee |
|-----------------|-----|
| $250,000 - $499,999 | $900 |
| $500,000 - $999,999 | $2,500 |
| $1,000,000 - $4,999,999 | $6,000 |
| $5,000,000+ | $11,790 |

## Employment Development Department (EDD)

### Payroll Tax Requirements

#### CA Payroll Taxes
| Tax | Rate | Wage Base (2024) | Who Pays |
|-----|------|------------------|----------|
| UI (Unemployment) | 1.5% - 6.2% | $7,000 | Employer |
| ETT (Employment Training) | 0.1% | $7,000 | Employer |
| SDI (State Disability) | 1.1% | $153,164 | Employee |
| PIT (Personal Income Tax) | Varies | All wages | Employee |

#### Filing Requirements
- [ ] **DE 9** - Quarterly Contribution Return
- [ ] **DE 9C** - Quarterly Wage Report
- [ ] **Due Date** - End of month following quarter
- [ ] **e-File/e-Pay** - Required for 10+ employees

#### New Hire Reporting
- [ ] **Report within 20 days** of hire
- [ ] **DE 34** - New Employee Registry
- [ ] **Online:** https://eddservices.edd.ca.gov/tap/secure/eservices

### Paid Family Leave (PFL)
- [ ] Provide notice to employees
- [ ] Post required notices
- [ ] Up to 8 weeks of partial wage replacement

## California Department of Tax and Fee Administration (CDTFA)

### Sales & Use Tax

#### Registration
- [ ] **Seller's Permit** - Required if selling tangible goods
- [ ] **No Cost** - Free to register
- [ ] **Display** - Permit must be displayed at business location

#### Filing & Payment
| Annual Tax Liability | Filing Frequency |
|---------------------|------------------|
| $0 - $25,000 | Annual or Quarterly |
| $25,001 - $100,000 | Quarterly |
| $100,000+ | Monthly with prepayments |

#### Current Rates (Fresno Area)
- **State Rate:** 7.25%
- **Fresno City/County:** Additional local rate
- **Total (varies):** Check exact location rate at CDTFA

#### Exemptions to Track
- [ ] Resale purchases (resale certificate)
- [ ] Food (generally exempt)
- [ ] Manufacturing equipment (partial)
- [ ] Shipping charges (if separately stated)

## Department of Industrial Relations (DIR)

### Cal/OSHA Requirements

#### General Safety Requirements
- [ ] **Injury & Illness Prevention Program (IIPP)** - Written program REQUIRED for ALL employers
- [ ] **Safety Training** - Document all training
- [ ] **OSHA 300 Log** - Maintain if 10+ employees
- [ ] **Heat Illness Prevention** - Required for outdoor workers

#### Cal/OSHA Postings
- [ ] Cal/OSHA poster
- [ ] Emergency phone numbers
- [ ] Access to Medical Records notice
- [ ] Whistleblower protections

#### COVID-19 Requirements (Check current status)
- [ ] COVID Prevention Program (if still required)
- [ ] Outbreak protocols
- [ ] Testing/notification requirements

### Division of Labor Standards Enforcement (DLSE)

#### Wage & Hour Requirements
- [ ] **CA Minimum Wage (2024):**
  - 26+ employees: $16.00/hr
  - All employers (2024): $16.00/hr
  - *Check for annual increases*

- [ ] **Overtime Requirements:**
  - Over 8 hours/day: 1.5x
  - Over 12 hours/day: 2x
  - 7th consecutive day: 1.5x (first 8 hrs), 2x (after 8 hrs)

#### Meal & Rest Breaks
| Requirement | Rule |
|-------------|------|
| Meal Break | 30 min unpaid before 5th hour |
| Second Meal | Before 10th hour (if working 10+ hours) |
| Rest Break | 10 min paid per 4 hours worked |
| Premium Pay | 1 hour pay if break missed |

#### Wage Statements (Pay Stubs)
Required information:
- [ ] Gross/net wages earned
- [ ] Total hours worked (non-exempt)
- [ ] Hourly rates and hours at each rate
- [ ] All deductions
- [ ] Pay period dates
- [ ] Employee name & last 4 SSN
- [ ] Employer name & address
- [ ] Sick leave balance (or on separate document)

#### Final Wage Requirements
| Termination Type | When Due |
|-----------------|----------|
| Fired/Laid Off | Immediately |
| Quit (no notice) | Within 72 hours |
| Quit (72+ hr notice) | Final day |

## California Civil Rights Department (CRD)
*Formerly DFEH*

### FEHA (Fair Employment & Housing Act)
*Applies to 5+ employees*

- [ ] **Protected Classes** - Race, color, religion, sex, gender, sexual orientation, marital status, national origin, ancestry, disability, medical condition, genetic information, military/veteran status, age (40+)
- [ ] **Sexual Harassment Training:**
  - Supervisors: 2 hours every 2 years
  - Non-supervisors: 1 hour every 2 years
  - New hires: Within 6 months
- [ ] **DFEH Poster** - Post "California Law Prohibits Workplace Discrimination and Harassment"

## California Privacy (CCPA/CPRA)

### Consumer Privacy Rights
*Applies to businesses meeting thresholds*

#### Thresholds (meet one):
- Annual gross revenue $25M+
- Buy/sell/share personal info of 100,000+ consumers/households
- 50%+ revenue from selling personal information

#### If Applicable:
- [ ] Privacy policy on website
- [ ] "Do Not Sell My Info" link
- [ ] Respond to consumer requests within 45 days
- [ ] Employee privacy notices

---

## Workers' Compensation Insurance

### Requirements
- [ ] **Mandatory Coverage** - Required for ALL employers with employees
- [ ] **Posting** - Post "Notice to Employees" poster
- [ ] **Claim Form** - Provide DWC-1 within 1 business day of learning of injury
- [ ] **MPN** - Medical Provider Network selection

### Penalties for Non-Compliance
- Misdemeanor criminal offense
- Civil penalty up to $100,000
- Stop order preventing business operation
