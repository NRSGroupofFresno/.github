# PIRL Project Definitions

The term "PIRL project" refers to several distinct initiatives, depending on the context. This document provides an overview of the most common meanings.

## Research & Academic Projects

### Partnership for Inclusive Research and Learning (PIRL)
A network of researchers and practitioners who advocate for Disability Inclusive Development (DID) research. The project focuses on the processes and tools required to build inclusive research teams that respectfully and equitably include researchers living with disabilities, particularly in low-resource settings.

### Public Interactives Research Lab (PIRL)
Based at the University of Texas at Dallas, this lab explores, builds, and analyzes interactive communications technologies deployed in public or shared spaces. Projects have included interactive art installations and historical timelines.

### Product Innovation Research Laboratory (PIRL)
This initiative, notably at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, brought together faculty and students from design, marketing, business, and engineering to solve product development problems for industry clients.

### Poverty & Inequality Research Lab (PIRL)
This lab conducts collaborative, interdisciplinary research to inform policy related to poverty, housing, education, and families, with projects such as studies on housing abandonment in Baltimore City.

## Government & Technology

### Participant Individual Record Layout (PIRL)
The U.S. Department of Labor (DOL) uses this term to refer to a mandated reporting layout for participant data in workforce development programs. It provides a standardized set of data elements and definitions for federal reporting requirements.

### Persistent Programming In Real Life (PIRL)
This refers to a series of workshops and meetings for software development leaders interested in programming methodologies for persistent memories.

## Other Uses

### Pirl (PIRL) Cryptocurrency
Pirl is a specific cryptocurrency that users can generate through mining.

### PIRL as an Acronym in Performance Appraisal
In some contexts, PIRL stands for **Performance, Image, Results, and Learning** as a framework for employee evaluation and development.
